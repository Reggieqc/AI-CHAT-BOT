# Company Knowledge Base

## Refund Policy

Customers can request a refund within 7 days of purchase.
The product must be in its original condition.
Digital products are eligible only if less than 20% has been consumed or accessed.
Refunds are processed within 5 business days after approval.
Refund requests must include a valid order ID and the email used during purchase.

## Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped.
If the order is already shipped, a cancellation request cannot be processed.
Once cancelled, the customer will receive a full refund.

## Shipping Policy

Standard delivery time is 3 to 5 working days.
Express delivery takes 1 to 2 working days.
Shipping delays can occur due to holidays or regional issues.
Customers receive a tracking ID via email once the order is shipped.

## Customer Support

Support hours are 10 AM to 6 PM IST, Monday to Saturday.
The average response time is 2 hours during support hours.
Customers can contact support via:

- Email: support@example.com
- Live chat on the website
- Phone: +91-90000-12345

## Product Warranty

Most electronics come with a 1-year warranty covering manufacturing defects.
The warranty does not cover physical damage, water damage, or unauthorized repairs.
Customers may initiate warranty claims through the support portal.

## Account & Login Issues

If a customer cannot log in:

1. They should reset their password using the “Forgot Password” option.
2. If the email is not received, they should check spam folders.
3. If the issue persists, contact support to verify account status.

## Payment Methods

We support the following payment methods:

- Credit and debit cards
- UPI
- Net banking
- Wallets (PhonePe, Paytm, Amazon Pay)
- International cards (Visa, Mastercard)

## Offers & Discounts

Discount codes must be applied at checkout.
Only one coupon can be applied per order.
Offers are not applicable on already discounted products unless specified.

## FAQs

**Q: Can I request a refund after 7 days?**
A: No, refund requests must be submitted within 7 days.

**Q: Do you offer replacement instead of refund?**
A: Yes, replacements are available for eligible products.

**Q: What if I entered the wrong delivery address?**
A: Contact support immediately. If the order is unshipped, the team can update it.

**Q: How long does it take to receive my refund?**
A: It usually takes 5 business days after the refund is approved.
